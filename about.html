I am a junior at Lehigh University pursuing an Integrated Degree in Engineering and Arts and Sciences (IDEAS), specializing in computer science and design. I have interests in UX/UI design, human-computer interaction, and tech policy.

IDEAS is an honors program in which students can essentially “build their own degree” across many fields of study. Problem-solving has always been a talent of mine and in combination with my creative nature and passion for social justice and politics, my degree was born.

Over the years, I’ve watched the internet and all of its potential come to life. As a member of Generation Z, I have an intimate relationship with and understanding of social media – what it is, what it does, and how it has made me feel and behave personally. The essay I wrote and submitted in my application to Lehigh was actually about my tumultuous relationship with social media and the anxiety it had given me in high school.

Now that I’m a little older, and I’d like to think a little wiser, I can not only analyze and understand social media and the internet, but also my feelings towards it and my behaviors as a result of it. With this understanding and background in computer science and design, I want to share my knowledge with others and provide the best online experience possible because it is possible.
